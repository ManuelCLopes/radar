# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want this project to be useful, direct, and safe for contributors of different backgrounds and experience levels.

Examples of expected behavior:

- be respectful and technically constructive
- assume good intent, but challenge ideas with evidence
- focus on reproducible problems and concrete improvements
- accept feedback without personal attacks

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, threats, or discriminatory language
- doxxing or sharing private information without permission
- trolling, deliberate disruption, or bad-faith review behavior
- sexualized language or unwelcome advances

## Scope

This applies to GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews, and other project spaces.

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer privately:
[github.com/ManuelCLopes](https://github.com/ManuelCLopes)

The maintainer may remove content, restrict participation, or ban contributors when necessary to protect the project and its community.
